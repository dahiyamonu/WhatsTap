About WhatsTap


WhatsTap is an open-source React-based project that streamlines the process of initiating a conversation on WhatsApp without needing to save the contact's number in your phone. Users can simply enter a phone number in the app, click "chat," and be directed straight to the WhatsApp chat window without adding the number to their contacts.
